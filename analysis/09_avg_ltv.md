# Average Customer Lifetime Value

## Key Metric
- Average LTV: 2,279.58

---

## Interpretation
- On average, each customer generates ₹2,279.58 over their lifetime.
- This indicates a strong revenue contribution per user across the entire base.

---

## Business Insight
> The current average LTV provides a solid foundation for growth.

- Revenue extraction per user is substantial, driven by the cohort of long-term subscribers.
- However, this average is heavily skewed by high-tenure users; the high-risk segment identified earlier (42% churn) still represents a significant revenue leak.

---

## Risk Assessment
- While the overall average LTV is healthy, the high-churn segments restrict the company’s ability to:
  - Maximize the return on acquisition costs.
  - Scale aggressively without losing nearly half of the new Fiber optic customer base.

---

## Recommendation
- Increase average customer lifetime further through retention strategies focused on the 1-year mark.
- Improve pricing or upsell opportunities for the loyal 2-year contract base.

---

## Impact
- Even small improvements in tenure for the month-to-month segment can significantly increase the total average LTV.
- Directly improves unit economics and long-term sustainability.
